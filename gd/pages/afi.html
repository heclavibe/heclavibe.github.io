AFI Film Festival 2022

For the American Film Institute I was hired for two months as a Senior Graphic Designer. I worked directly with the creative director on the AFI on 2022 branding and marketing campaing for the annual Film Festival.

I co-created a design language for the festival which was a plethera of multi-media. Including social media ads, video ads, online ads, signage, venue collateral and posters.
